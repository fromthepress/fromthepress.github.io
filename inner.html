General Information.

Rewind Supply Company is a sportswear brand targeted primarily at actionsports and lifestyle enthusiasts. All of our designs are printed on American made clothing. In fact, all production takes place in Los Angeles, California.

Wholesale Policy

  • A minimum of $200 is required for all initial wholesale orders.
  • Restocks from then on require a minimum of $100.
  • In order to open a wholesale account with us, you must be associated with an established retail location, distribution company, or online storefront.
  • We reserve the right to accept or deny any applications.
  • We reserve the right to revoke access to your wholesale account at any time. (If there is a credit on the account or a pending order it will be refunded immediately.)
